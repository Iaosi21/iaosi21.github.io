How to unlock the oneui 8 bootloader

Requirements: a FIB (Focused Ion Beam) machine, a extreme microscope

Please note that these are specialized lab equipment and very expensive (around 1 million dollars. maybe less on ebay). I recommend you buy used machines.

Step 1:

locate the fuses on the die using the microscope. you would see a little fuse bank (this is very hard to do)

Step 2: Using the FIB

Mill away the metal layer, Apply ion beam to blow or cut a fuse (Warning: this would need a exact schematic, hard to get also one mistake could destroy nearby circuits) also I'm not sure which efuse is for the bootloader.

Step 3: Verification

Reassemble the device and boot it up. if it booted up then it's a good sign.
